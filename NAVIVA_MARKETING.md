- **Özgürlük**: Denizde özgür seçim yapabilme hakkı

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Inter Bold / Poppins (strong, modern)
- **Body**: Inter Regular (clean, readable)
- **Mono**: Fira Code (code blocks, technical specs)
- **Size Scale**: 12px (caption) → 48px (hero)

### Design Language
- **Glassmorphism**: Semi-transparent cards, frosted glass effect
- **Spacing**: 8px grid system (consistent breathing room)
- **Radius**: 8-16px (modern, not aggressive)
- **Shadows**: Subtle elevation (depth without distraction)
- **Icons**: Emoji + Lucide React icons (accessible, emoji-safe)

## 🎁 Growth & Acquisition Strategy

### Phase 1: Soft Launch (Months 1-3)
**Goal**: 500 active users, product-market fit validation
- Boat club partnerships (free premium for members)
- Direct outreach to sailing schools
- Turkish boating subreddit, Facebook groups
- Referral program: Davet et, 1 ay premium kazan
- **Budget**: Content creation, influencer seeding, community management

### Phase 2: Public Launch (Months 4-6)
**Goal**: 5,000 active users, press coverage
- AppStore + Google Play launch campaigns
- PR campaign in Turkish tech/boating media
- Influencer partnerships (micro-influencers, sailing creators)
- LinkedIn B2B outreach for masters/craftspeople
- **Budget**: Paid ads (Google App Campaigns, Instagram/Facebook), PR firm, content

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 7-12)
**Goal**: 20,000 active users, revenue generation begins
- Performance marketing (App Install ads, retargeting)
- Sailing community events and sponsorships
- Premium feature upsell (GPS tracking, collaborative planning)
- International expansion exploration (Greece, Croatia, etc.)
- **Budget**: Performance marketing, events, product team growth

## 💰 Monetization Strategy

### Freemium Model

#### Free Tier (Forever Free)
- ✅ Tekne yönetimi (14 modül, temel özellikler)
- ✅ Seyir günlüğü ve navigasyon
- ✅ Usta bulma ve mesajlaşma
- ✅ 5 GB depolama
- ✅ Mobil + web senkronizasyon

#### Premium Tier ($4.99/month or ₺150/month)
- 🚀 GPS tracking gerçek zamanlı
- 🚀 İşbirlikçi sefer planlama (crew invites)
- 🚀 Gelişmiş analitik ve raporlar
- 🚀 Danışman destek (email/chat)
- 🚀  100 GB depolama
- 🚀 Reklamsız experience
- 🚀 Hazırlanıyor: Sigorta entegrasyonu

#### Marketplace Commissions
- **Usta Listeleri**: ₺50-500/ilan (one-time listing fee, optional)
- **Transaction Fee**: 5-10% on service bookings (future feature)

### Revenue Projections (Year 1)
- **Users**: 20,000 monthly active
- **Premium Conversion**: 10% = 2,000 paying users
- **Premium Revenue**: $100k/year
- **Marketplace**: $20k/year (listing fees + commissions)
- **Total Y1**: ~$120k (pre-optimization)

- **CAC** (Cost per Acquisition): <$2
- **Monthly Growth**: 50% MoM (first 6 months)
- **Download Target**: 50k by end of Y1
- **DAU/MAU Ratio**: 40%+ (engagement)

### Monetization
- **Premium Conversion**: 10-15%
- **LTV** (Lifetime Value): >$50/user
- **LTV/CAC Ratio**: >25:1 (healthy SaaS)
- **Marketplace GMV**: $20k → $100k by Y1 end

### Product
- **Feature Adoption**: 80% of users engage with ≥3 modules
- **Session Duration**: 10-15 min average (desktop), 5-8 min (mobile)
- **NPS Score**: >50 (benchmark: 40+ is good)
- **Retention**: Day 7: 60%, Day 30: 40%, Day 90: 25%

### Community
